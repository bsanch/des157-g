Interactive Audio Visualizer

This project focuses on the combination of and relationship between audio and visuals. The combination of the two create synaesthesia, the stimulation of senses. Some users are able to see sound in shapes and color, and I hope that this project allows them to explore the two senses through browser-based interaction. Those who produce music, enjoy listening to music, and are interested in visuals/graphics are the target audience.

It encourages creativity, appeals to synaesthesia, and also provides a way for artists to visualize their audio. The goal is to allow users to visualize their voice and custom audio. When finished creating, they can share their creations online with other users.

Move mouse left and right to adjust volume of your audio.
To create a recording, click 'Record.' To save, click 'Stop recording' and then 'Save audio.'
